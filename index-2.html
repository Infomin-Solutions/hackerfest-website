ABOUT THE EVENT:

Problem-solving events are opportunities for individuals or teams to demonstrate their critical thinking and creativity in addressing complex issues. Participants are challenged to identify problems, develop effective plans, and implement solutions that can produce desirable outcomes. Such events come in various forms and attract diverse groups of participants. They provide an avenue for promoting innovation and strategic thinking, while also building valuable skills and networks.

One such great problem-solving event is HACKERFEST, which is centered around coding and software development. Participants are expected to have a strong understanding of technical requirements, such as programming languages. This knowledge is crucial for ensuring that the software they create is efficient, scalable, and meets the desired specifications.

HACKERFEST encourages participants to showcase their programming skills, experiment with new technologies, and collaborate with others to solve challenging problems.It offers a unique opportunity for individuals to apply their knowledge and expertise in a practical setting, while also fostering creativity, innovation, and collaboration.

The Entrepreneur Discussion with Students is a special event , a forum that brings successful entrepreneurs and aspiring students together to inspire and educate students about entrepreneurship.It is an excellent opportunity for students who are interested in entrepreneurship to learn from successful entrepreneurs and to build their networks. It is an inspiring and educational forum that can help students to discover their entrepreneurial potential and to take steps towards achieving their goals
